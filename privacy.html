While using our Service, we do not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information on our own servers. However, the Service uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Third-Party Service Providers

We provide paid products and/or services within the Service. In that case, we use third-party services for payment processing (e.g., Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

We will not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to our third-party payment processors whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

  • Apple Store In-App Payments: Their Privacy Policy can be viewed at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/

Cozy Sheep Farm requires a persistent internet connection to function. This connection is used to verify game data, process store transactions, and load advertisements.
